Sindh

Sindh a province of Pakistan, is rich in crafts from ancient times. The major crafts include block printing, Ajrak, tie and dye, fine cottons and muslins, such as Sussi and Khes, Farasi (goat hair rugs) embroidery, Rilli (applique or patch work), bangles, jewellery and lacquer work. Other crafts include kaashi (pottery), soosi (textile), Sindhi topi (cap) and jhundi (furniture). Balochistan

Balochi handicrafts are handicraft or handmade crafted works originating from Baloch people. [1] Baloch handicrafts is not merely a craft; it serves as a means of cultural identity and expression. Each design often carries symbolic meanings related to Baloch traditions and beliefs.
